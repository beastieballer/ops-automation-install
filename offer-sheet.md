## Example Version 1 Offer
# Inbox + Job Admin Agent

A workflow that monitors incoming activity and turns inbox / portal chaos into a usable operating summary.

### Inputs
- Gmail inbox
- job portals
- calendar
- invoice or payment systems
- spreadsheets / Notion / admin trackers

### What the workflow does
- scans for new activity
- classifies messages: lead, client, vendor, payment, update
- extracts key details such as:
  - customer or job
  - location
  - timeline
  - issue or request
  - next step
- flags urgent items
- prepares draft replies
- groups tasks by what needs action next
- generates a daily or recurring ops summary

### Example outputs
- Today’s Ops Summary
- Needs Reply
- Needs Scheduling
- Needs Payment Follow-Up
- Low Priority / Archive
